Peperium

Historical meme project about pepes, on the Ethereum blockchain.
⭐️ First project to utilize IPFS

When you see a green checkmark ✅ that means there's an IPFS image available that matches with the on-chain ipfs hash. When you see a lock that means the asset is locked and the supply is finite. If not, it might be possible the asset owner can mint more
